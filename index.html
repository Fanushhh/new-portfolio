About Me

My journey into web development started purely out of curiosity. I was amazed by the websites and applications I saw online and was driven to learn how they were made. Two years later, I still enjoy every second of it. While it can be overwhelming at times, the feeling of figuring out how things work behind the scenes and putting them into practice is incredibly rewarding.

I believe every project must have the end-user in mind, and I always focus on making sure the user gets exactly what they need as quickly as possible. To achieve this, I use a wide range of technologies. On the front-end, I'm proficient with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, along with frameworks like React and Next.js. I have experience with various styling solutions, including Tailwind CSS, SASS, styled-components, and Bootstrap. On the back-end, I'm experienced with Node.js and databases like MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and Redis.

I'm a firm believer in continuous learning. To deepen my understanding of core principles and data structures, I'm currently taking Harvard's CS50 course. My goals for this year are to become proficient in TypeScript and to start learning both automated and manual testing to ensure the quality and reliability of my work.
